The ultimate alliance

Perfect Plus is a premium quality cement specially engineered PPC Cement from the house of MP Birla Cement, the best cement company in India for specific applications like foundation, pillar and roofing works. The product technology has multiple advantages in improving the performance & life of the concrete. The highest quality standards have unique characteristics to make high-performance superior-grade concrete. Perfect Plus is available in Delhi (NCR), Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Haryana, Punjab, Gujarat, West Bengal, Jharkhand and Bihar.
